ABOUT

MEDIA TECHNOLOGIES

I'm Kristopher Kohlmeier, a recent Computer Science graduate working part-time managing, running, and maintaining the stage A/V equipment at Crossroads Church, located in Camarillo CA. Currently, I'm building my software engineering skills to pursue a career in CG animation technology. Animated films were a big part of my childhood, and to this day I'm inspired by great stories that are told through animation. In my spare time, I listen to music, build custom keyboards, bake, and play co-op and social games.

Initially I started as a volunteer at Crossroads and enjoyed the oppourtunity to work on the technology behind the scenes of a production. As a result, I taught myself A/V production by reading the manuals for the equipment, and used online resourses to learn best practices and standards for live productions. Since taking on this role I've played a key role in training volunteer teams and elevating weekly production quality with new hardware and software installs, while maintaining and repairing old A/V equipment.
